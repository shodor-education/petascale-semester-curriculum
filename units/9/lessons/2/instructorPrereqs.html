Before teaching this lesson, the instructor should have:

  • Familiarity with compiled languages.
  • Some knowledge of C/C++.
  • Familiarity with principles of loop optimization as per 4.1 and 4.4 from https://cnx.org/contents/u4IVVH92@5.2:bEZZukPR@1/Introduction-to-the-Connexions-Edition (Severance and Dowd). This is a classic text on optimization--and while some of the hardware examples are out of date as the text is now out of print, the theoretical descriptions of loop optimizations are all still valid.
