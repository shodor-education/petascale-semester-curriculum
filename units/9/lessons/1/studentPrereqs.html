Before starting this lesson, students should have:

  • Familiarity with the Unix/Linux Operating System.
  • Familiarity with the C language.
  • Familiarity with command line arguments and parsing.
  • Familiarity with pointers.
  • Familiarity with dynamic memory allocation.
  • Familiarity with looping constructs.
  • Familiarity with how to time a portion of executing code.
  • Familiarity with writing simple BASH scripts.
