ACM and IEEE have standards for Computer Science Education.

This lesson provides content, assessment, and direction for students in the following areas identified by the Standards committee.

  • AL - Algorithms and Complexity, page 55.
  • AR - Architecture and Organization, page 62.
  • CN - Computational Science, page 68.
  • GV - Scientific Visualization, page 82.
  • PD - Parallel and Distributed Computing, page 145.
  • PL - Programming Languages, page 155.
