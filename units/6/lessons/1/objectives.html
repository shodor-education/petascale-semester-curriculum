By the end of this lesson, students will:

  1. Review Distributed (MPI) and Shared Memory (OpenMP) computing, including understanding the pros and cons of each.
  2. Will be introduced to why and when it makes sense to combine the Distributed (MPI) and Shared Memory (OpenMP) computing together into a hybrid code.
  3. Learn Various Advantages, Pitfalls and Coding Strategies for Hybrid OpenMP/MPI coding
