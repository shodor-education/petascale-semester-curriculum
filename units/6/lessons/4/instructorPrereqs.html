Before teaching this module, the instructor should have:

  • Access to a compute nodes with/without GPU or workstations with/without GPU accelerators for testing and verifying MPI and OpenMP examples and exercise.
  • Understanding of parallel architectures and what kind of scientific applications could benefit from them.
  • Check with the HPC community any new releases and changes in MPI and OpenMP and make necessary modifications to the examples and exercises.
  • Familiarity with Fortran and C base compilers.
  • Understand how to use ImageMagix.
