By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

  • Discuss parallel programming and performance modeling basics that aids in executing application to given specifications, terminate normally.
  • Describe Parallel Computer Memory Architectures.
  • Discuss some factors that lead to performance degradation in parallel programs.
  • Discuss Amdahl's Law and Gustafson's Law.
  • Discuss the difference between strong scaling and weak scaling.
