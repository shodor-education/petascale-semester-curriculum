Before teaching this lesson, the instructor should have:

  • Access to compute nodes with/without GPU or workstations with/without GPU accelerators for testing and verifying the examples and exercises.
  • Understanding of ARGB pixel frames and what kind of scientific applications could benefit from movie loops.
  • Checked with the visualization community about any new releases and changes in ImageMagik and made necessary modifications to the examples and exercises.
  • Familiarity with Fortran and C base compilers - understand what a direct address file is in Fortran and how they work, likewise for C files.
