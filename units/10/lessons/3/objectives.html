Learning Objectives

After finishing this lesson students will be able to:

  • Describe what a scientific library is.
  • Describe how they are built.
  • Explain what their applications are.
  • Discuss the examples of popular scientific libraries:
    • PETSc
    • HDF5
    • NetCDF
    • Python libraries
  • Implement the application’s illustration of scientific libraries.
