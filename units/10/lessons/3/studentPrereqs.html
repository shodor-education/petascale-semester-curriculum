Before starting this lesson, students should have:

  • Either access to a cluster/supercomputer or workstation/personal computer with multicore processors and/or an interactive operating region or a "non-batch" compute zone.
  • Installed PGI Community Edition of the toolkit on their workstation. It's cross platform, so works on Windows, Mac, and Linux machines with GPU.
  • Intermediate-Beginner level experience with Fortran and C/C++.
  • Understanding of bit-level manipulations at the binary level, and two's complement arithmetic.
  • Access to ImageMagik.
