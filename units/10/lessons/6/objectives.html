By the end of this lesson, students will:

  • Define ARGB.
  • Knowledge of what is .png file and the ARGB format, how it works, and its use cases.
  • How to convert an array floats into scaled integers.
  • Understand the file formats in Fortran and C.
  • How to construct an alpha, red, green, blue 32 bit pixel.
  • Learn by example how to avoid bad practices in Fortran/C programming that could lead to potential performance issues and data representations.
  • Hands-on examples and exercises for building scientific applications with animations.
