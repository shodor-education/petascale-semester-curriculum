1. The instructor must acquire experience in programming in the C language, knowledge of OpenMP, and how to utilize a parallel computer system.
  2. The instructor must understand and experience the shared memory concepts of OpenMP, the practices and the applications.
  3. The instructor has some working program examples for showing how the OpenMP of applications and practices work.
  4. The instructor has access to the multicore computer with C and OpenMP installed.
  5. The instructor provides every student in the course access to the multicore computer with C and OpenMP installed.
