By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

  • Demonstrate understanding of the N-body problem.
  • Demonstrate understanding of the basics of Newtonian mechanics, gravity (used in this problem) and Verlet's method to integrate the differential equation.
  • Successfully determine where OpenMP #pragmas should be placed to parallelize the code.
  • Demonstrate understanding of why the nested acceleration loops are the most important to parallelize.
