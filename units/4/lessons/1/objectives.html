After completing this lesson, students will be able to do the following:

  • Define a race condition and give an example where it occurs in everyday life
  • Use the parallel for pragma of OpenMP to parallelize for loops
  • Identify and resolve the following types of race conditions in parallel for loops:
    • A variable that should not be shared between iterations (resolved by adding a private declaration to the pragma or moving the variable declaration into the loop)
    • A variable used in a reduction operation (resolved by adding a reduction pragma)
