By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

  • Recall different codes written using MPI for various scientific applications in engineering, biology, etc.
  • Recall real life scientific computations and open source codes of some existing examples such as: numerical solver (Jacobi, Poisson, ...); in biology: Smith-Waterman and pandemic simulation codes; materials science simulations.
  • Recall a tour of codes that are being used in science and how they are distributed.
  • Know the main fields that use MPI to solve problems and how MPI touches different fields of n-body and pandemic.
