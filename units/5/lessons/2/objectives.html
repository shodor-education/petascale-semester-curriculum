By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

  • Have a better understanding of collective communication concepts and modes.
  • Understand concepts of binary tree and butterfly structures for computation and distribution of work among process.
  • Examine characteristics of different collective communication routines provided by MPI library such as:
    • Barrier Synchronization.
    • Broadcast, Scatter, Gather, etc.
    • Global sum, global max/min, etc.
