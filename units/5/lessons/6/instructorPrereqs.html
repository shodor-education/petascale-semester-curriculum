Before teaching this lesson, the instructor should have:

  • Intermediate knowledge of the C programming language.
  • Access to a C compiler with MPI installed: gcc or clang.
  • Completed the lessons:
    • Introduction to MPI
    • Collective vs. Point-to-Point Communication
    • Performance Evaluation of MPI Programs
  • Desired but not required:
    • Access to an MPI cluster.
    • Knowledge how to send tasks for the MPI cluster.
