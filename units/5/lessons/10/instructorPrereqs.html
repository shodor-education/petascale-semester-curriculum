Before teaching this lesson, the instructor should have:

  • Access to compute nodes or workstations for testing and verifying examples and exercises using MPI.
  • Understanding of parallel architectures and what kind of scientific applications could benefit from them.
  • Checked with the HPC community for any new releases and changes in MPI and OpenMP and made necessary modifications to the examples and exercises.
  • Familiarity with Fortran and C base compilers.
  • Understanding what an IP address is and how an ethernet switch works.
  • Understanding of how to use ImageMagik.
