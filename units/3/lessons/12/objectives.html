In this lesson, students will acquire the following abilities:

  • Students will understand the basic information required to queue a job
  • Students will be able to determine what computing resources are necessary for a parallel job
  • Students will learn how to write job files, submit and execute a job with a given script to run on the resource manager & job scheduler (SLURM).
  • Since TORQUE is the resource Manager and Job scheduler installed and used on Blue Water System, one example will be tested with it as well.
