Before teaching this lesson, the instructor should have:

  • Familiarity with Flynn's taxonomy.
  • Familiarity with vector architectures.
  • Familiarity with loop unrolling.
  • Familiarity with matrix multiplication.
  • Familiarity with Foster's methodology (see: Designing and Building Parallel Programs, by Ian Foster, available at: https://www.mcs.anl.gov/~itf/dbpp/ ) for designing parallel programs.
