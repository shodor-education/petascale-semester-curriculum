Before starting this unit, students should have:

  • A laptop or desktop computer.
  • A high-speed internet connection and ready access to a multi-core, multi-CPU Linux or Unix computer, preferably a high-performance computing cluster, with at least 256 MB of total memory and at least 1 GB of disk space.
  • Access to—and a rudimentary understanding of—the C and Python programming languages, a C compiler, GNU make, MPI library (mpicc and mpirun), VisIt software, and, if necessary, minimal experience with job scheduling (slurm or PBS).
  • A solid understanding of introductory physics, thermodynamics, and some familiarity with differential equations.
