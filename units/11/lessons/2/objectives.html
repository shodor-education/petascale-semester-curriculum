By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

  • Install the VisIt software package.
  • Understand the difference between finite-element and finite-volume simulations.
  • Use the VisIt software to visualize and analyze a complex, finite-element dataset: blood aneurysm data and analysis courtesy Gilles Fourestey and Jean Favre, Swiss National Supercomputing Centre, and Lawrence Livermore National Lab.
