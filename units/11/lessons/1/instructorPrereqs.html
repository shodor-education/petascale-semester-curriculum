Before teaching this lesson, the instructor should have:

  • Familiarity with basic concepts and applications behind the following simulation methods:
    • Density Functional Theory
    • Molecular Dynamics(MD)
    • Monte Carlo(MC)
    • Mesoscopic Coarse Grained MD/MC
    • Lattice Boltzmann Method
    • Continuum Conservation Equation solvers
  • Familiarity with the concept of machine learning.
  • Understanding of the diffraction limit of light microscopy, in order to motivate the need for computation by explaining the technological limitations of experimental equipment.
