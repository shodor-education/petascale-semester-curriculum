It is highly recommended that students use a laptop or desktop computer to complete each lesson. It is highly recommended that students have a high-speed internet connection and ready access to a multi-core, multi-CPU Linux or Unix computer, preferably a high-performance computing cluster, with at least 256 MB of total memory and at least 1 GB of disk space. Students should have access to and a rudimentary understanding of the C and Python programming languages, a C compiler, GNU make, MPI library (mpicc and mpirun), and if necessary, minimal experience with job scheduling (slurm or PBS).
