Before teaching this lesson, the instructor should have:

  • C compiler, GNU make, Python, MPI library, VisIt software.
  • Access to a multi-core, multi-CPU computer with at least 1 GB disk storage.
  • Knowledge of C, MPI, mpicc, mpirun, VisIt visualization and analysis.
  • Understanding of fluid dynamics, thermodynamics, astrophysics, and differential equations.
