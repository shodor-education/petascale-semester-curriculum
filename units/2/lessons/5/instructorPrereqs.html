Before teaching this lesson, the instructor should have:

  • Familiarity with shared memory.
  • Familiarity with distributed memory.
  • Familiarity with basic networking and network topologies common in high-performance computing (point-to-point, ring, crossbar, toroidal mesh, 2D planar mesh, and 3D planar mesh).
  • Basic computer interconnect performance and resiliency metrics including bisection bandwidth and how to model traffic flow/identify bottlenecks.
