Before teaching this lesson, the instructor should have:

  • Familiarity with data parallelism.
  • Familiarity with task parallelism.
  • Familiarity with basic computer program design.
  • Familiarity with basic computer programing algorithms.
  • Understanding of galaxy formation (n-body problem), building several computers (assembly line), matrix multiplication, Gaussian elimination, and finding the area under a curve. Here enough background is needed to identify data and task parallelism and lead a discussion about those topics.
