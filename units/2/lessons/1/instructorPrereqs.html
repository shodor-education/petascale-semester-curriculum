Before teaching this lesson the instructor should be familiar with the following topics:

  1. Data parallelism
  2. Task parallelism
  3. Basic computer program design
  4. Basic computer programing algorithms
  5. Understanding of galaxy formation (n-body problem), building several computers (assembly line), matrix multiplication, Gaussian elimination, and finding the area under a curve. Here enough background is needed to identify data and task parallelism and lead a discussion about those topics.
